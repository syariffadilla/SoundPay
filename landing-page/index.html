Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SoundPay

What is SoundPay?
SoundPay is a decentralized payment platform that uses zero-knowledge proofs and blockchain technology to enable private, fast, and low-cost transactions.
How does SoundPay ensure privacy?
SoundPay uses zero-knowledge proofs (ZK-proofs) to validate transactions without revealing any sensitive information about the parties involved or the transaction details.
What blockchain does SoundPay use?
SoundPay is built on the Sui blockchain, known for its high throughput, low latency, and secure environment for decentralized applications.
Is SoundPay secure?
Yes, SoundPay utilizes advanced cryptographic techniques and blockchain technology to ensure the highest level of security for all transactions.
