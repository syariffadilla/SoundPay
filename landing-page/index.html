Revolutionary DeFi Features

Built for privacy, security, and efficiency in the decentralized economy

zkProof Privacy

Complete transaction privacy using zero-knowledge proofs. No sensitive data is ever revealed while maintaining full verifiability.

Micro-transactions

Ultra-low fees enable micro-payments and small transactions that were previously uneconomical on traditional networks.

Smart Escrow

On-chain escrow system protects both buyers and sellers. Funds are released only when predefined conditions are met.

Analytics Dashboard

Track your payment history with proof of authenticity. Complete transparency while maintaining privacy.

WALRUS Storage

Verifiable proofs stored on decentralized WALRUS storage, ensuring immutability and global accessibility.

Instant Settlement

Lightning-fast transactions with immediate finality. No waiting for confirmations or network congestion.

How SoundPay Works

Simple interface, powerful technology underneath

1

Initiate Payment

User creates a payment request with amount, recipient, and optional escrow conditions through our intuitive interface.

2

Generate zkProof

Soundness Layer generates zero-knowledge proof for transaction validation without revealing sensitive details.

3

Execute & Store

Transaction executes on blockchain with proof stored on WALRUS. Instant settlement with full verifiability.
